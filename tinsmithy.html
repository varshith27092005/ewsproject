Description: Tinsmithy involves the fabrication of thin metal sheets into various items such as containers, funnels, and other sheet metal products.

Apparatus Used:

  • Snips
  • Sheet Metal
  • Mallet
  • Stakes
  • Soldering Iron
  • Shears
  • Hand Seamer

Process:

  1. Measure and mark the sheet metal according to the project plan.
  2. Cut the sheet metal using snips or shears.
  3. Bend and shape the metal using stakes and mallets.
  4. Join pieces together using soldering or riveting as required.
  5. Finish the product by smoothing edges and cleaning.
