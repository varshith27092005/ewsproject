Description: Welding is a fabrication process that joins materials, usually metals or thermoplastics, by causing coalescence. This is often done by melting the workpieces and adding a filler material to form a pool of molten material that cools to become a strong joint.

Apparatus Used:

  • Welding Machine
  • Electrodes
  • Welding Helmet
  • Welding Gloves
  • Welding Apron
  • Wire Brush
  • Chipping Hammer
  • Angle Grinder

Process:

  1. Prepare the metal surfaces to be joined by cleaning them.
  2. Set up the welding machine with the appropriate settings.
  3. Wear protective gear.
  4. Strike an arc and move the electrode along the joint to form a weld bead.
  5. Allow the weld to cool, then clean the slag with a chipping hammer and wire brush.
