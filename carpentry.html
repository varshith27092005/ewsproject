Description: Carpentry involves the cutting, shaping, and installation of building materials, particularly wood, to construct structures and objects.

Apparatus Used:

  • Hand Saw
  • Chisels
  • Hammer
  • Nails
  • Workbench
  • Clamps
  • Drill
  • Sandpaper

Process:

  1. Measure and mark the wood according to the project plan.
  2. Cut the wood to the required dimensions using a hand saw.
  3. Shape and smooth the wood using chisels and sandpaper.
  4. Assemble the pieces using nails and a hammer.
  5. Finish the product by sanding and applying any necessary treatments.
