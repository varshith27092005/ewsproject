House Wiring Experiment

Back to Home

Description: House wiring involves the installation of electrical circuits and systems within residential buildings.

Apparatus Used:

  • Wire Strippers
  • Screwdrivers
  • Pliers
  • Voltage Tester
  • Electrical Tape
  • Circuit Breakers

Process:

  1. Plan the electrical circuits and layout.
  2. Turn off the power supply.
  3. Install wires, switches, outlets, and fixtures according to the plan.
  4. Test the circuits for proper connections and safety.
  5. Label the circuits and restore power.
